Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak German to study in Germany? expand_more
No! Many universities offer programs entirely in English, especially at the Master's level. We can help you find these programs.
How much does the consultation cost? expand_more
The initial consultation is completely free. We discuss your goals and eligibility before moving forward with any paid services.
Can I work while studying abroad? expand_more
Yes, most student visas (like in Germany, UK, and Australia) allow you to work part-time (usually 20 hours/week) during semesters and full-time during breaks.
